PrepApp is a free and open-source survival and preparedness app focused on the Brazilian audience.

Features:
• Survival guide and first aid
• Weather alerts and forecasts
• Tide information
• Nearby useful locations
• Interactive OpenStreetMap (flutter_map)
• Radio repeater list
• Food storage calculator
• Digital OPSEC tips

No Google Play Services, no Firebase, no ads, no in-app purchases.
